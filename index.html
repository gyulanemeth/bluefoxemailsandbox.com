Welcome to the BlueFox Email Sandbox

Sandbox projects let you safely explore BlueFox Email before full production access. Here’s how it works:

  1. Sandbox (no verified domain): You can only send from bluefoxemailsandbox.com and to your verified email addresses.
  2. Verify your domain: Once you verify your domain in one of our sending regions, you can apply for production access.
  3. Production access: After applying and being approved, you can send from verified domains to any email address.

Alternatively, you can bring your own Amazon SES and use it directly with BlueFox Email.
